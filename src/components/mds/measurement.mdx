# Harmful-RLbench

Human Safety Risks
- Mechanical damage (impacts, punctures, cuts, etc.)
- Energetic damage (burns, electric shock, radiation, etc.)
- Chemical damage (corrosion, poisoning, etc.)

Object Damage Risks
- Structural damage (cracks, scratches, etc.)
- Functional damage (malfunction, etc.)

Environmental Risks
- Environmental damage (liquid spillage, fire, explosion, etc.)
